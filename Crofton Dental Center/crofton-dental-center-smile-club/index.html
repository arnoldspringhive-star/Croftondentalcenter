Dental Savings

Crofton Dental Center Smile Club

We believe that every patient should have access to the highest quality dental care at affordable prices and without the hassles and restrictions of insurance. Therefore, we are pleased to offer our Smile Club membership program that gives patients control of their oral health.

Premium Coverage

In-House Savings Plan

Affordable annual membership giving you immediate access to comprehensive preventative dental care.

Plan Inclusions:

  • ✨ 2 Professional Cleanings per year
  • ✨ 2 Comprehensive Exams per year
  • ✨ 2 Fluoride Treatments per year
  • ✨ Routine Dental X-Rays (as needed)
  • ✨ 1 Emergency Exam (with necessary X-rays)
  • ✨ 15% Off all other treatments*

*Some exclusions apply. Please contact our office coordinator for full details.

Exclusive Membership Benefits:

  • ✔️ No deductibles to pay
  • ✔️ No claim forms to file or manage
  • ✔️ No pre-authorization requirements
  • ✔️ No pre-existing condition limitations
  • ✔️ No waiting periods — coverage starts immediately
  • ✔️ Family Discount: Additional 10% discount for families of 3 or more (children up to age 18)
  • ✔️ Whitening Deal: 25% discount on in-house Zoom Whitening, including custom trays

Smile Club vs. Dental Insurance

Unlike conventional dental insurance, the Smile Club puts you in complete control. There are no corporate claims adjusters determining what treatment you can or cannot receive. Your care plan is designed directly by you and Dr. Rahat Sood, DMD based on your health and comfort needs alone.
