Common Questions on General Care

Regular check-ups allow us to detect signs of tooth decay, gum disease, and oral cancer early when they are easiest to treat. Professional cleanings remove calcified plaque (tartar) that cannot be brushed away at home.

For most patients, we recommend visiting Dr. Sood twice a year (every six months) for cleanings and exams. Patients with active periodontal (gum) issues may require more frequent visits to control bacteria levels.
