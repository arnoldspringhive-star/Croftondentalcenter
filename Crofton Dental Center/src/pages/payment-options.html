Insurance, Financing, & Payment Options

We want to make your dental care as stress-free as possible. We offer a variety of payment methods, file insurance claims on your behalf, and provide flexible payment options.

💳

Dental Insurance

We accept and file claims with most major dental insurance providers. Our staff works closely with insurers to maximize your coverage benefits and minimize your out-of-pocket costs.

📊

Flexible Financing

For larger procedures or cosmetic treatments, we partner with CareCredit to offer low monthly payment options, including interest-free plans for qualified patients.

Apply via CareCredit

Other Accepted Payment Methods

For your convenience, we accept cash, personal checks, and major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover). Payments are due at the time of service unless other arrangements are pre-arranged with our office coordinator.
