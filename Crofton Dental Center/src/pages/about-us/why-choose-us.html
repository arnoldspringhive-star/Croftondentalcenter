Our Philosophy

Why Choose Crofton Dental Center

We believe dental visits should be something you look forward to. We've built our practice around core pillars that ensure a premium, comfortable experience for every patient.

🏡

A True Family Atmosphere

We are an independent family dental practice, not a corporate clinic. We take the time to know you, your family, and your personal smile goals, creating long-term relationships built on mutual trust.

🎓

Experienced & Celebrated Expert

Dr. Rahat Sood, DMD graduated with high honors from Boston University and has been recognized as a TOP Dentist throughout Anne Arundel and Prince Georges Counties. Her interdisciplinary expertise means you get specialized care under one roof.

🛋️

Deep Compassion & Sedation Options

Dental anxiety is real, and we handle it with absolute compassion. We offer nitrous oxide and oral conscious sedation, alongside cozy comforts like warm blankets, neck pillows, and complimentary water.

🔬

State-of-the-Art Technology

We leverage cutting-edge technology including digital X-rays, 3D CBCT imaging for implant placement, iTero digital scanners (no messy molds), and advanced sterilization chambers for the highest precision and safety.

🐾

Active Community Engagement

We don't just work in Crofton—we live here and give back. Through Zoey’s Legacy (Anne Arundel County SPCA) and our Annual Food Drive (Crofton Food Bank), we partner with our patients to make AA County stronger.
