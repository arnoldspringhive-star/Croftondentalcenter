Our Patient Journey

The Crofton Experience

We have re-imagined the entire dental appointment from start to finish. Discover what to expect when you choose Crofton Dental Center for your healthcare.

1

Effortless Scheduling

Book your appointment via phone, or use our simple, online appointment request drawer. We coordinate check-ins and send text reminders to fit your busy calendar.

2

A Warm Welcome

Step into our cozy lobby and receive a friendly greeting from Lindsay or Mr. Sood. Enjoy a fresh bottle of water and settle into comfortable seating—there is never a rushed corporate clinic feel here.

3

Cozy Amenities & Sedation

Once in our operatories, wrap yourself in a warm blanket, use a soft neck pillow, and choose nitrous oxide or oral conscious sedation if you feel any dental anxiety.

4

Advanced Diagnostics

Dr. Sood performs evaluations using 3D CBCT imaging and iTero scanners, avoiding uncomfortable traditional goopy impressions and ensuring highly detailed treatment precision.

5

Collaborative Decisions

We discuss all your diagnostic information and outline clinical options together. We details the financial information upfront so you can make informed decisions about your smile.

6

Continuous Post-Care

Following treatments, we check in on your healing progress, send helpful post-care instruction packets via email, and schedule your next wellness cleanings to ensure continuous oral health.
