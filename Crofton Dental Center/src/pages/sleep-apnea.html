Oral Appliance Therapy (OAT)

Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) occurs when the soft tissues in the back of the throat collapse, blocking oxygen flow and causing gasping or heavy snoring.

Instead of a bulky mask and noisy CPAP machine, we create custom **Mandibular Advancement Devices (MAD)**. These small appliances shift your lower jaw forward slightly, keeping the airway open and allowing you to breathe quietly and wake up refreshed.

  • ✔️ Quiet Sleep: Eliminates snoring so your partner can sleep too.
  • ✔️ Travel-Friendly: Small enough to fit in your pocket, with no cords or filters.
  • ✔️ High Comfort: Custom-fitted to sit comfortably over your arches.
