Relaxing Sedation Methods

We offer two safe and highly effective dental sedation methods to match your level of comfort needs:

  • 🎈 Nitrous Oxide (Laughing Gas): A mild sedative inhaled through a nose mask. It keeps you calm and happy, and wears off in minutes so you can drive home safely.
  • 💊 Oral Conscious Sedation: A prescription pill taken shortly before your appointment. You remain fully awake and cooperative, but experience deep relaxation and have little memory of the visit. *(Requires a companion driver)*.
