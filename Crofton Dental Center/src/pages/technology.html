Clinical Precision

Advanced Dental Technology

We invest in the latest dental diagnostic and treatment technology to provide highly precise, safe, and comfortable procedures. Dr. Sood and the team use state-of-the-art tools to achieve optimal patient outcomes.

📐

3D CBCT Digital Scans

Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) provides a highly detailed 3D view of your teeth, bone structure, and nerve pathways. This allows Dr. Sood to perform highly precise 3D-assisted dental implant placements and diagnose root problems with supreme accuracy.

📸

iTero Digital Scanner

Forget about messy, goopy putty molds. Our iTero digital scanners capture highly precise 3D color images of your teeth in minutes. This digital model is used to design custom crowns, bridges, and Invisalign clear aligners with a perfect fit.

🩻

Low-Radiation Digital X-Rays

Our digital X-ray sensors capture instant images on our monitors while emitting up to 90% less radiation than traditional film X-rays. This makes your diagnostics safer, faster, and environmentally friendly.

🫧

Modern Sterilization Centers

Your safety is non-negotiable. Our practice uses hospital-grade autoclaves and advanced sterilization chambers that undergo rigorous daily biological monitoring to guarantee absolute cleanliness and infection control.
